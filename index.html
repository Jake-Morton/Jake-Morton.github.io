About

Service

The work and services I provide include:

Design Build- This is a process in construction that gets rid of any challenges within cost and schedule that allows for a much smoother construction process that makes the project flow smoother and be completed at a quicker rate.

Construction Management- It can be difficult to have a construction process where there is no coordination or communication between the owner, architect, and construction manager so the company makes it our responsibility to have meetings with the owner as well as the architect to make sure that everyone is on the same page for the construction process to make sure everything is done in an orderly manner and professionally.

General Contracting- As a General contractor the company makes sure that we manage people to be the best they can be so they can be more efficient and effective. When the company completes projects we make sure to do it professionally to make sure that you as an owner would want us back for another project.
